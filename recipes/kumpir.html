Kumpir Recipe

Description

Kumpir is a popular Turkish street food that consists of a baked potato stuffed with a variety of fillings. It’s known for its crispy skin and soft, creamy interior. The dish is highly customizable, allowing you to add ingredients like butter, cheese, olives, sausages, corn, and various salads to suit your taste.

This hearty and delicious dish is perfect as a main meal or a side and is easy to prepare at home. Follow this simple recipe to make your own Kumpir, and enjoy a taste of Turkey in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes
  • 100g butter
  • 200g grated cheese
  • 100g sweet corn
  • 100g sliced olives
  • 100g sliced sausage (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Various salads (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 200°C (392°F).
  2. Wash the potatoes thoroughly and pat them dry.
  3. Place the potatoes on a baking tray and bake for 1-1.5 hours until the skin is crispy and the inside is soft.
  4. Once baked, cut the potatoes lengthwise, but not all the way through, creating a pocket.
  5. Fluff up the interior of the potatoes with a fork and mix in the butter and grated cheese until well combined.
  6. Add your chosen fillings, such as sweet corn, olives, sausage, and salads, into the potato.
  7. Season with salt to taste, and serve hot.
