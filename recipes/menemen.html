Menemen Recipe

Description

Menemen is a traditional Turkish breakfast dish made with eggs, tomatoes, green peppers, and spices. It's similar to scrambled eggs but with a richer, more flavorful taste thanks to the vegetables and olive oil. Menemen is quick and easy to prepare, making it a perfect choice for a hearty breakfast or brunch.

This dish is typically served with fresh bread to soak up the delicious sauce. You can also add ingredients like onions, cheese, or sausages to customize the dish to your liking.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 2 green peppers, sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and saut√© until soft and translucent.
  3. Add the green peppers and cook for 3-4 minutes until they start to soften.
  4. Stir in the diced tomatoes and cook until they break down and form a sauce, about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Crack the eggs into the pan and gently stir to combine with the tomato mixture.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes, and cook until the eggs are set to your liking.
  7. Serve hot with fresh bread.
