Lahmacun Recipe

Description

Lahmacun, also known as "Turkish pizza," is a delicious flatbread topped with a flavorful mixture of minced meat, tomatoes, onions, and spices. It’s a popular street food in Turkey, often enjoyed with a squeeze of lemon and a sprinkling of fresh herbs. Lahmacun is thin, crispy, and packed with flavor, making it a perfect snack or meal.

Traditionally, it's served rolled up with fresh salad inside, but you can also enjoy it flat. It's easy to make at home, and you can experiment with different toppings to suit your taste.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground lamb or beef
  • 2 medium tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green pepper, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Flatbread or thin pizza dough
  • Lemon wedges for serving
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 220°C (428°F).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground meat, chopped tomatoes, onion, green pepper, tomato paste, garlic, paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Roll out the flatbread or pizza dough into thin rounds.
  4. Spread a thin layer of the meat mixture over each piece of dough.
  5. Place the lahmacun on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes until the edges are crispy and the meat is cooked through.
  6. Remove from the oven and serve hot with lemon wedges and fresh parsley if desired.
